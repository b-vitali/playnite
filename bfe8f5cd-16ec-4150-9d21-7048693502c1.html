Description

An adorable puzzle game about being a monster and making snowmen

Every hour, the monster goes out to the weather station in London to check the temperature. They set the price accordingly:

14.7°C = $14.70!

The normal price is $14.99, so this is 2% off.

FAQ

Why can't I buy the Steam/iOS/Android/Switch version at this price?
Unfortunately we can't automatically change the pricing on other platforms, so this is just available on itch.io for now.
Does my purchase include a Steam key?
Steam keys are often resold on dodgy second-hand marketplaces that we don't want to support. Because the game will commonly be available very cheaply or for free, unfortunately Steam keys are not included.
Why are you doing this?
Introducing this as a permanent price model is our really stupid way of celebrating the game's 10 year anniversary.
Who made this game?
We're the puzzle experts Draknek & Friends - you might know us from A Monster's Expedition (which also features the same monster from A Good Snowman) or our publishing projects like Bonfire Peaks and LOK Digital.
We also organise the annual Steam event Cerebral Puzzle Showcase and have a monthly newsletter of thinky puzzle game recommendations, Thinky Third Thursday.
We have a new game coming soon, also starring the monster. Please check out The Electrifying Incident!
Where/when is the temperature checked?
The temperature is checked in London using OpenWeather, once an hour.
What if the temperature is below 0°C?
Ha ha, nice try. The game will be free.
I use Fahrenheit though!
Okay, if you insist. 14.7°C is 58.46°F, so you can set the payment amount to $58.46 in the checkout process.
I use Kelvin though!
We greatly appreciate your generosity! 14.7°C is 287.85°K, so you can set the payment amount to $287.85 in the checkout process.
